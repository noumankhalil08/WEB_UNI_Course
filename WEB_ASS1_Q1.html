Cheese Sandwich Recipe:

Ingredients:

  1. Bread slices2
  2. chicken250gm
  3. Cheddar Cheese
  4. Mayonise
  5. Ketchup
  6. BBQ Sauce
  7. Toaster
  8. chilli flakes

Instructions:

First you have to marinate the chicken adding chiili, salt ,tumeric and lemon. After that, take 2 bread slices add mayonise on base then ,add cooked marinated chicken then add two slices of cheddar cheese then add ketchup and BBQ sauce according to your Taste . At Last, add chilli flakes for topping and then toast your Sandwich in baking oven or in Toaster based on Availaibility for 5 minutes. Enjoy yor Delicious Cheese chicken Sandwich . Thanyou
